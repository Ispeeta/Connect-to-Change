About Connect To Change:

Personal Note: As a teenager, it's no doubt that we have the most access to information due to the amount of technology that's incorporated in our lives. Yet, with all this information and "news," it can be overwhelming learning about what's happening in the world. As a result, many people still do not know/understand situations happening around the globe.

However, I also do know that teenagers nowadays are dedicated to learning and standing up for what's right. Connect to Change is created by a teenager, maintained by teenagers and is FOR teenagers, for all of us to become better global citizens.

Student journalism is a fantastic outlet for young kids, and it's beneficial for all readers. It needed to be more broadcasted and available, so that is why I created this website. The "campaigns" feature is there to shine a light on specific issues that are prevalent in our society and history.

All in all, Connect to Change is here to help others be aware of the world around us.
